Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and let them sit without stirring for about 1 minute.
  4. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, then add fillings if using.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and serve immediately.
